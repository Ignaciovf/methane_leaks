Why Methane Matters

Methane traps heat in the atmosphere over 80 times more effectively than carbon dioxide in the short term. Large leaks from landfills, pipelines and industrial sites accelerate climate change and harm nearby communities.

Our mission is to spotlight the biggest emitters so regulators, companies and citizens can act quickly. By making invisible emissions visible, we help drive accountability and mitigation.

Data Source

We leverage data from the United Nations Environment Programme's International Methane Emissions Observatory. Their global monitoring program brings transparency to methane pollution.
